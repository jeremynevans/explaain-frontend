Apple vs FBI

The FBI has managed to hack into the iPhone of one of the San Bernadino gunmen without the help of Apple, who were refusing despite a court order.

The FBI announced on 22 March it had partnered with an unnamed third party to gain access to the iPhone without Apple's help.
